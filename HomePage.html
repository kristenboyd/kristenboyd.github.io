The Makeup Test

My goal here is to simply review and suggest products that I use to makeup lovers all over. I know when I shop for makeup I tend to refrain from certain products if I don't know how well the apply or look on my skin complextion. In which, helped me come up with the idea for this website. However, most of the products I suggest works for MY skin-complection and skin-type. Therefore, everything I use should work well for people with combination skin and a darker skin complection. Seeing that I dont get too in-depth about certain shades/colors, then most of these products could work just as well with other skin-types and complections.
