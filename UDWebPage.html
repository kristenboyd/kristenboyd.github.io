My Top 5 Urban Decay Products

PREP SPRAY

B6: Vitamin-Infused Complextion Prepping Spray

This soothing spray preps and balances the skin, while evening out skin tone, absorbing oil, and reducing shine. This great to use first thing in the morning or even right before bed.

EYES

NAKED Eyeshadow Palette

The NAKED Palette has 12 bronze-hued shadows. My favorite colors shades are half-baked, buck, and smog. All 12 colors are really pigmented, but using an eyeshadow base will make these colors pop even more.

HIGHLIGHTER

Afterglow

Afterglow has a soft and creamy texture that applies easily and goes on sheer and lasts 8 long hours.

CONCEALER

Naked Skin: Weightless Complete Coverage Concealer

Has full coverage, lasts all day (including hot summer days), and worth the money. It's amazing for its coverage and doesn't dry too fast, which allows you to blend as you please.

SETTING SPRAY

De-Slick: Oil Control Makeup Setting Spray

This spray helps keep makeup just the way you put it on, without having to reapply for oily spots.
