My Top 5 Nyx Products

EYEBROWS

Mirco Brow Pencil

This eyebrow pencil works wonders. Not only is it only $10, but it works like it should be $100. NYX has another brow pencil that is a $3-4 less, but it doesn't work that well and tends to come on darker.

EYES

Eyeshadow Base - Skin Tone

This eyeshadow base is honeslty one of the best, espcaially for the price. I recommend the the skin tone base for people of color, but the white pearl eyeshadow base can work just as well.

LASHES

Double Stacked Mascara

Any mascara can add length or volume, but this one is a combination of both. The only downside to this is that you will have two separate bottles of mascara.

LIPS

Soft Matte Lip Cream

50% of the lipsticks I own (including solids, creams, and liquids) come from the soft matte collection. My favorite are Dubai, Copenhagen, and Monte Carlo

BLUSH

Baked Blush - Ignite

Although I had an allergic reaction to this blush, it was still very flattering. This blush tends to look better in the summer time, because it gives off a nice warm glow.
